Chapter One

The Weight of Glass

Charlotte Gordon Lennox had exactly fourteen minutes before the world she'd built came crashing down around her.

She stood at the floor-to-ceiling windows of her corner office on the thirty-second floor, watching the November rain streak across Manhattan like mascara on a beautiful face. Below, Fifth Avenue pulsed with its usual orchestra of yellow cabs and black town cars, all of them carrying people who still had their lives intact.

Her phone buzzed again. The third time in two minutes. She didn't need to look to know it was Marcus, her CFO—former CFO, she corrected herself, since there was no company left to be chief financial officer of.

Fourteen minutes. That was all the time between Charlotte and the meeting that would decide whether she walked out of this building with her dignity or was carried out by security.

The Crypto King's Muse

by Francesca Frost

Charlotte Gordon Lennox doesn't have time to mourn her losses. Once a high-flying fashion entrepreneur, she's now buried in debt, hustling for survival in the volatile world of cryptocurrency. When a job at Riverbanka powerful crypto platformpromises financial salvation, she grabs it. But there's a catch.

One of Riverbank's enigmatic cofounders is Duane Blacklock, a brilliant, secretive CEO known throughout the industry as the Crypto Kingand whose path once collided with Charlotte's in a way she's never forgotten or forgiven.

Now, working side by side, their chemistry is undeniablebut so are the shadows they both carry. As Charlotte rises inside Riverbank, she's drawn deeper into Duane's gravity: his brilliance, his obsession, his ghosts from a violent past. And as their worlds collide, she becomes the museand the weaknessof a king who will risk everything to keep her.

From Manhattan to the Cayman Islands to the glittering chaos of Davoswhere the line between control and surrender blurs, and love becomes the most volatile trade of all.
